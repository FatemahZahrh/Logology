Questions & answers

Is the logo removed from the catalog after I buy it?

The idea of Logology is to offer a designer logo at an affordable price when you're starting out ($69 and $119 packages). In these cases, the logo won't be removed from the catalog. With the more expensive $599 option however, we make a unique variation of the logo, so you're the only one to have it.

Our idea is that when you're just getting started, it's more important to have something that looks professional but stays within your budget.

Can I trademark my logo?

With the "establish" logo package, you get a unique design with full exclusivity and trademarking rights. You can then apply for a trademark within your country's jurisdiction.

What if I made a mistake in my order?

If you made a mistake in your brand name, color, or font choice, we're happy to fix it at no additional cost. Just email us at support@logology.co.

Can I resell or distribute the logo elsewhere?

f you are a designer or agency that needs to resell to your customers, please contact us at support@logology.co for a custom contract. We do not currently allow reselling or distributing on other platforms.
